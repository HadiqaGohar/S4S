# Prompt Improvement Assignment

## Step 1: Original Prompt

**Prompt:**

```text
Write a business plan for a bakery.
```

---

## Step 2: Improved Prompt

**Prompt:**

```text
Write a detailed business plan for a small neighborhood bakery that specializes in fresh bread, cakes, and pastries. Include the following sections:

1. Executive Summary
2. Business Description
3. Market Analysis (target customers, competitors, and industry trends)
4. Products and Services
5. Marketing and Sales Strategy
6. Operations Plan (location, staffing, suppliers, and equipment)
7. Financial Plan (startup costs, monthly expenses, revenue projections, and break-even analysis)
8. Risk Assessment and Mitigation Strategies
9. Growth Plan for the first three years

Assume the bakery will be located in a mid-sized city with a startup budget of $100,000. Present the plan in a professional format using clear headings, bullet points where appropriate, and realistic assumptions.
```

---

## Step 3: Explanation of Changes

* Specified the type of bakery to make the prompt more focused.
* Added required sections for a complete business plan.
* Included business assumptions such as location and startup budget.
* Requested detailed financial information.
* Added risk assessment and a three-year growth plan.
* Specified formatting with headings and bullet points to improve readability.

---

## Step 4: Comparison of Outputs

### Original Prompt Output

The original prompt generated a basic business plan containing the main business sections such as the executive summary, business description, products and services, market analysis, marketing strategy, operations plan, management team, financial plan, and conclusion.

### Improved Prompt Output

The improved prompt generated a much more detailed and professional business plan. It included structured financial projections, startup costs totaling $100,000, monthly expenses, break-even analysis, competitor analysis, industry trends, risk assessment, mitigation strategies, and a three-year growth plan. The response was better organized and more suitable for real business planning.
