# Leave Request Email Task (Gemini)

## Step 1 · Prompt Given

Write a professional email from me (a junior developer) to my manager requesting 2 days of leave next week for a family wedding. Keep it under 100 words, polite but not overly formal.

---

## Step 2 · Gemini Response

**Subject:** Leave Request: [Dates] – [Your Name]

Hi [Manager's Name],

I hope you’re doing well.

I’d like to request two days of leave next week, from [Start Date] to [End Date], to attend a close family wedding.

Ahead of my time off, I’ll ensure all my current tasks are up to date and hand over any urgent items to the team. I will also be reachable via Slack/email for emergencies.

Thank you for understanding!

Best regards,

[Your Name]

Junior Developer

---

## Step 3 · Edited Version

The sentence:

> "Ahead of my time off, I’ll ensure all my current tasks are up to date and hand over any urgent items to the team."

was revised to:

> "I will complete my work before my leave and hand over any urgent tasks to the team."

### Final Email

**Subject:** Leave Request for Two Days

Hi [Manager's Name],

I hope you’re doing well.

I’d like to request two days of leave next week, from [Start Date] to [End Date], as I need to attend a family wedding.

I will complete my work before my leave and hand over any urgent tasks to the team. I’ll also be available on Slack/email if anything important comes up.

Thank you for your understanding.

Best regards,

[Your Name]
