**Project:** Student Task Management Mobile App
**Team Size:** 5 Members

| Time      | Agenda Item               | Lead            | Discussion Items                                      | Decision Items                         |

| 20–25 min | Development Plan & Tools  | Lead Developer  | Tech stack, GitHub workflow, sprint structure         | Confirm tools and development process  |
| 25–28 min | Testing & Risk Assessment | QA Engineer     | Testing strategy, possible risks, quality standards   | Approve testing approach               |
| 28–30 min | Wrap-Up & Next Steps      | Project Manager | Review decisions, assign tasks, answer questions      | Confirm deadlines and responsibilities |

1. MVP features approved.

3. Sprint schedule finalized.
4. Testing strategy approved.
5. First-week tasks assigned to all team members.
