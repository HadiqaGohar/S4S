# 🧪 Creativity Control Experiment (Z.ai)

## 🎯 Objective
To compare how AI responds to low-creativity and high-creativity prompts, and observe differences in vocabulary, originality, and risk-taking.

---

## 🪜 Steps

### Step 1 · Action
Open Z.ai.

Website:
https://chat.z.ai

---

### Step 2 · Action
Use the low-creativity prompt:

> Give me a safe, conventional, predictable tagline for a tea brand.

### Response

**"Comfort in every cup."**

---

### Step 3 · Action
Open a new chat and use the high-creativity prompt:

> Give me 5 wild, absurd, highly unconventional taglines for a tea brand — the kind a surrealist poet would write.

### Response

1. "Boil the shadow of a clock; drink the geometry of sleep."

2. "A hallucination of wet dirt, served in a cup made of your grandmother's whisper."

3. "For when your throat craves the precise weight of a melting accordion."

4. "We pressed a million tiny screams into a bag—just add water and swallow the void."

5. "An amber puddle where moonlight goes to drown its parasitic twin."

---

### Step 4 · Action
Compare the vocabulary and risk level of both sets.

#### Low-Creativity Response
- Simple vocabulary
- Safe and brand-friendly
- Easy to understand
- Low creative risk
- Suitable for mainstream marketing

#### High-Creativity Responses
- Highly imaginative vocabulary
- Surreal and poetic imagery
- Unpredictable wording
- High creative risk
- Memorable but not suitable for most commercial brands

---

### Step 5 · Action
Pick which tagline you would actually use for a real brand.

### Choice

**"Comfort in every cup."**

### Reason

I would use **"Comfort in every cup."** because it is clear, memorable, easy to understand, and suitable for a wide audience. While the surreal taglines are creative and interesting, they are too abstract for a real tea brand and may confuse customers.

## 🧠 Key Insight

Prompt wording strongly influences AI output. A simple prompt produces safe and predictable responses, while a creativity-focused prompt encourages unusual vocabulary, metaphorical thinking, and higher-risk ideas.

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

AI can adjust its creativity level based on instructions. Clear prompting allows users to control whether outputs are practical and conventional or imaginative and experimental.
