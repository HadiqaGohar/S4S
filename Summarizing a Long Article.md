## Step 3 · Prompt Given to Kimi

"Summarise this article in 5 bullet points, then give me a 2-sentence TL;DR at the end."

### Kimi AI Response

#### Summary (5 Bullet Points)

- Most adults read 1000 words in about 5 minutes at 200 WPM, though speeds range from 3.3 minutes (fast readers) to 10 minutes (slow readers).
- Reading speed varies depending on purpose, with studying requiring slower reading and skimming allowing much faster reading.
- Reading aloud takes longer than silent reading, typically 6–8 minutes for 1000 words.
- Factors such as text difficulty, formatting, distractions, language proficiency, and fatigue affect reading speed.
- Reading time can be estimated using the formula Word Count ÷ WPM, with extra time added for pauses and comprehension.

#### TL;DR

The average person reads 1000 words in about 5 minutes, but the actual time depends on reading speed, purpose, and text complexity. Using your reading speed and context provides a more accurate estimate of reading time.
