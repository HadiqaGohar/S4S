#### Summary (5 Bullet Points)

- Most adults read 1000 words in about 5 minutes at 200 WPM, though speeds range from 3.3 minutes for fast readers to 10 minutes for slow readers.
- Reading speed changes depending on the purpose, such as studying, casual reading, or skimming.
- Reading aloud takes longer than silent reading, usually requiring 6–8 minutes for 1000 words.
- Factors such as text difficulty, formatting, distractions, language proficiency, and fatigue can affect reading speed.
- Reading time can be estimated using the formula Word Count ÷ WPM, with additional time allowed for pauses and comprehension.

#### TL;DR

The average person reads 1000 words in about 5 minutes, but actual reading time varies depending on reading speed, purpose, and content difficulty. Using your reading speed and reading context provides a more accurate estimate.
