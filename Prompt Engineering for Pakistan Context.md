# Step 4: How Local Anchors Reshaped the Answer

The localized prompt significantly changed the quality of the response by adding local context. Instead of producing a generic marketing plan, the AI created a campaign specifically designed for Eid in Lahore. It incorporated a fixed budget of Rs. 100,000, targeted women aged 22–40, mixed Urdu and English captions, and included one WhatsApp status idea for each of the seven days. The recommendations also focused on Lahore-based audiences, local influencers, and delivery services, making the plan much more practical and relevant.
