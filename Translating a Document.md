Karobar, sehat, taleem aur muwasilat ke shobon mein technology ne beshumar aasaniyan paida ki hain. Taham is ke kuch manfi asraat bhi hain, jaise waqt ka zaya, social media ka had se zyada istemal aur privacy ke masail.
