# 🧪 Few-Shot Prompting Experiment

## 🎯 Objective
To observe how AI learns patterns from examples and predicts outputs for unseen inputs.

---

## 🪜 Steps

### Step 1 · Action
Open ChatGPT.

---

### Step 2 · Action
Type a prompt with 2 worked examples followed by a new input:

Input: Apple → Output: Fruit  
Input: Dog → Output: Animal  
Input: Rose → Output: ?

---

### Step 3 · Action
Send and read the answer.

**AI Response:**
Flower

---

### Step 4 · Action
Try again with a harder pattern:

Product: iPhone → Tagline: Think Different  
Product: Nike → Tagline: Just Do It  
Product: Coca Cola → Tagline: ?

---

### Step 5 · Action
Observe how the AI mimics the unseen pattern.

**AI Response:**
Coca Cola → Open Happiness

---

## 📊 Evidence (Shareable Proof Link)

https://chatgpt.com/share/6a3b8ed7-0ffc-83e9-9f26-1d979b67afb7

---

## 🧠 Observation

- AI correctly learned the pattern in Step 2 (classification task).
- AI followed branding pattern in Step 4 (tagline prediction).
- Multiple valid answers exist depending on training data.

---

## 📌 Key Insight

Few-shot prompting works because the AI:
- Learns patterns from examples
- Predicts the most likely continuation
- Uses probabilistic reasoning instead of fixed answers

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

Providing examples significantly improves AI performance in pattern-based tasks.
