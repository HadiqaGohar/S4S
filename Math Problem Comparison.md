# Discount and Tax Calculation Task

## Step 1 · Problem Statement
A shop gives 20% off, then applies 5% sales tax. Final price of a Rs. 2000 item? Show every step.

---

## Step 2 · AI Solutions
Both AI tools (ChatGPT and DeepSeek) solved the problem using step-by-step reasoning.

---

## Step 3 · Self Solution (Paper Work)

### Original Price:
Rs. 2000

### Step 1: Apply 20% Discount
Discount = 20% of 2000  
= (20/100) × 2000  
= 400  

Price after discount = 2000 − 400 = **1600**

![Math Problem Comparison](https://github.com/HadiqaGohar/S4S/blob/main/Math%20Problem%20Comparison.jpeg?raw=true)

---

### Step 2: Apply 5% Sales Tax
Tax = 5% of 1600  
= (5/100) × 1600  
= 80  

Final Price = 1600 + 80 = **1680**

---

### Final Answer:
**Rs. 1680**
